About Us

Background

We are Hemlock, a website created by four Stony Brook University computer science undergraduate students. This site is produced for non-commercial purposes.

This site replicates Rotten Tomatoes, where a user is allowed to view movie and TV show information and ratings. The user can create an account to review movies for themselves, and add movies and TV shows of their choice to the watchlist or blacklist.

This project was a part of the Spring 2018 CSE 308 curriculum.

Certified Fresh Picks

For both movies and TV Shows, the admins select Certified Fresh films that we think are exceptional, and deserve a spotlight.

The Rating System

We have 3 different ratings per movie, the Critic Rating, Audience Rating and the Average Rating. The critic rating is based on the average of all critics that reviewed the movie, while the audience review is the average of all non-critics that reviewed the movie. The Average Rating is the combination of the two.

Hemlock Critics

Critics are approved by the admins of Hemlock, via applications. The applications are available to all users, and is able to apply to become a Critic at any time. However we hold all rights to reject the application as it seems fit.
